Description

These examples check if the value is a member of the NumMatrices using the isInside function.

 

Details

5 is not contained the NumMatrix.

(isInside 5 #(nummat| 1 6 7 3)) Returns: false

6 is contained in the NumMatrix.

(isInside 6 #(nummat| 1 6 7 3)) Returns: true
